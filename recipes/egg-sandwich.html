Egg-sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Greek yogurt
  • Mustard, pepper, salt and honey
  1. Put water to boil, after that, put the eggs and let them cook for 10 minutes
  2. After 10 minutes, peel the eggs and remove the yolks from the rest
  3. Mix the yolk and the greek yogurt until it turns one only mixture.
  4. Add 1 spoon of mustard, pepper, salt, and honey
  5. Serve with bread or any other food and enjoy
