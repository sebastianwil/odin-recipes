Lengua en salsa

Ingredients

  • Lengua 500mg
  • One leaf of Laurel
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  1. Clean and cook the lengua
  2. Remove it from the fire and then blend the tomatoes, chop the onion and after that make a sauce
  3. Add the sauce to the lengua, and the laurel leaf, let it cook for 20 mins
  4. Serve and enjoy
